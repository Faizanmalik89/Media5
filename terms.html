Community Guidelines

Last Updated: March 1, 2023

At Content Hub, we strive to create a positive, respectful, and enriching environment for all users. These Community Guidelines outline the standards of behavior we expect from our community members. By using Content Hub, you agree to comply with these guidelines.

1. Be Respectful

Treat others with respect and courtesy, even if you disagree with their opinions. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

2. Post Quality Content

Share content that is informative, educational, or otherwise valuable to the community. Content should be original or properly attributed if using external sources.

3. No Inappropriate Content

Do not post content that is:

  • Illegal or promotes illegal activities
  • Sexually explicit or pornographic
  • Violent or graphic
  • Promotes self-harm or harm to others
  • Misinformation or deliberately misleading

4. Protect Privacy

Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information about other users without their consent.

5. No Spam or Self-Promotion

Don't spam the platform with excessive self-promotion, advertisements, or repetitive content.

6. Respect Intellectual Property

Only share content that you have the right to share. Respect copyright, trademark, and other intellectual property rights.

7. Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it using the contact form. Our moderation team will review reports and take appropriate action.

8. Consequences of Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination, depending on the severity and frequency of violations.

These guidelines are not exhaustive, and Content Hub reserves the right to make determinations about what constitutes appropriate behavior on our platform. We may update these guidelines from time to time to address new issues or reflect changes in our practices.
