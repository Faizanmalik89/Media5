Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform

How do I submit content to the platform?

To submit content, you need to sign up for an account. Once logged in, navigate to the Blogs or Videos section and use the submission form at the bottom of the page. Note that only admin users can submit video content.

Is there a cost to use Content Hub?

No, Content Hub is completely free to use. You can read articles, watch videos, and even contribute your own blog content without any charges or subscription fees.

How can I become an admin to upload videos?

Admin privileges are granted by our team to select content creators. If you're interested in becoming a video content creator, please contact us with samples of your work and area of expertise.

Can I request specific topics for future content?

Absolutely! We welcome content suggestions. Use the contact form on this page to send us your ideas, and our team will consider them for future articles and videos.

How can I report inappropriate content?

If you come across content that violates our community guidelines, please use the contact form to report it. Include the URL and specific concerns, and our moderation team will review it promptly.
